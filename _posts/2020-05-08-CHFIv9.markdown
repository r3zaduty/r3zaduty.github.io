# What is CHFI?

Computer hacking forensic investigation is the process of detecting hacking attacks and properly extracting evidence to report

the crime and conduct audits to prevent future attacks.

Computer crime in today’s cyber world is on the rise. 

Computer Investigation techniques are being used by police, government and corporate entities globally 

and many of them turn to EC-Council for our Computer Hacking Forensic Investigator CHFI Certification Program.
